In order to initialize the program place the @SMATool folder on an easy to reach place on your computer and follow the steps below:

1. Start MATLAB
2. Go to the parent folder to @SMATool
3. Write SMATool in the MATLAB prompt.
4. Use the software.

Much more detailed descriptions of the usage and built in functions will appear here within a short time. Please be patient.
